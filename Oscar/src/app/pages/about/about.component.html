ABOUT us

ABOUT us

In 2008 Cefetra, a leading European trading company, established a market research desk with the aim of being able to forecast price direction in an increasingly complex world.

As we were not satisfied with what’s available on the market in terms of market research products and taking our passion, in combination with our unique quantitative and forward-looking approach, we developed our own to fill this crucial gap. In the last few years, we have experienced strong demand for our market research products.

To improve our service, we will distribute our standardized market research products under the brand name Ceras Analytics from 2022. We will be moving our research products to the Ceras Analytics platform from (late) 2022, an interactive and customizable dashboard developed using our extensive database and in-house expertise.

Our focus at Ceras Analytics is on forecasting price direction, rather than reporting the current status of the market. Over the past 10 years we have developed quantitative models that have enabled us to forecast the development of supply and demand (e.g., yield models) and more importantly, allowed us to translate these fundamentals into price forecasts.

As we believe in a quantitative approach, we focus on three major disciplines within our team: Data Engineers, Quants and Fundamental Market Analysts.

Our PURPOSE

Everyone within the European and Black Sea grains and oilseeds supply chain are exposed to risks in our market, however not everyone has the time or resources available to do their own in-depth market research.

It's our purpose to provide high quality, standardized research to the European and Black Sea grains and oilseed supply chain for a competitive price with a strong focus on market direction.
